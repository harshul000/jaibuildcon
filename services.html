Core Expertise

What We Build

From highways to bridges, institutional buildings to irrigation canals, our expertise spans every domain of civil infrastructure.

Highway Construction

We have extensive experience in construction of National and State Highways, expressways, bypass roads and flyovers. Our work includes subgrade preparation, base course, bituminous surfacing, drainage systems, and road safety features.

  • 2-lane to 6-lane widening projects
  • Rigid & flexible pavement design
  • Road safety and signage
  • Drainage & storm water management

Bridge Engineering

With over 50 major bridge projects completed, we are specialists in design and construction of bridges across rivers, canals, and rail lines. Our engineering team handles all from foundations to superstructure works.

  • RCC T-Beam & Box Girder Bridges
  • Railway Over-Bridges (ROBs)
  • Viaducts & Flyovers
  • Culverts & minor bridges

Building Construction

We undertake construction of government buildings, institutional complexes, staff quarters, hospitals, and commercial structures. Our team ensures compliance with all statutory approvals and codes.

  • Government & PWD buildings
  • Railway staff quarters
  • Institutional & commercial complexes
  • Interior finishing & MEP works

Earthwork & EPC

From large-scale earthwork for railway formations to complete engineering, procurement and construction (EPC) contracts, we handle the full project lifecycle with dedicated project management teams.

  • Embankment & formation works
  • Mass excavation & cutting
  • Turnkey EPC contracts
  • Irrigation canals & water networks
